The human body needs calories and other minerals

Calories, proteins, and essential minerals play a vital role in keeping your body healthy and functioning properly. Calories provide the energy needed for daily activities and bodily processes, while proteins are crucial for building and repairing muscles, tissues, and cells. Essential minerals like calcium, iron, and potassium support strong bones, oxygen circulation.
